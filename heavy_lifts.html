Heavy Lift Excellence

Engineering the Impossible

Carrying out a heavy lift transportation project starts with careful preparation and planning. At Aimco International, our project engineers develop the most economical, efficient and safest solutions for heavy lift projects.

With over 25 years of experience in complex lifting operations, we've successfully completed projects across six continents, handling loads from 100 to 5,000+ tons. Our commitment to precision engineering ensures every lift is executed flawlessly.

Thorough preparation is fundamental to our heavy lift project management. We begin with comprehensive risk assessment and feasibility studies to guarantee project success before mobilization begins.

Our Strategic Approach

Integrated Consultation

Our project preparation takes place in close consultation with clients, covering all aspects of HSE and technical requirements. We develop customized solutions that align with your operational objectives.

Technical Precision

Our engineers utilize advanced simulation software and 3D modeling to plan every aspect of the lift, ensuring structural integrity and operational safety under all conditions.

Safety First

Safety is our core value. We implement rigorous safety protocols, conduct comprehensive risk assessments, and ensure all personnel adhere to the highest safety standards.
