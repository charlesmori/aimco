Project Logistics

Expert handling of oversized and heavy-lift cargoes with precision and care

Complex Projects Demand Experience

Experience counts when it comes to handling complex projects and movements of oversized and heavy-lift cargoes.

Managing complex transportation for large scale projects requires experienced, intelligent and knowledgeable personnel who are committed to researching all options, investigating each detail, surveying and identifying potential obstacles (both physical and non-physical), and reacting to extraordinary circumstances with quick and decisive actions.

Aimco International Ltd specializes in project freight management and all associated planning and implementation services. Whether you're moving an individual heavy lift or complete industrial plant regionally, we arrange customized transportation concepts for the entire route on a door-to-door basis.

Our Project Logistics Approach

Strategic planning and execution for complex cargo movements

Strategic Breakdown

We break down project logistics and transport into essential elements, then assign direct supervision over each component to ensure nothing is overlooked.

Advance Reconnaissance

Our Projects Operations Specialists conduct advance trips to key points in the project corridor to gain first-hand knowledge required to anticipate and solve challenges.

Dedicated Coordination

A Project Coordinator is assigned to manage all facets of communication, logistics and transportation operations, serving as your direct liaison throughout the project.

Our Project Success Factors

The success of Aimco International lies in its commitment to understanding what is required. Our specialist advisers become fully conversant with the requirements of a project and ensure that:

  • Customer-specified deadlines are met without compromise
  • All Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) aspects are strictly followed
  • Special instructions and unique requirements are meticulously implemented
  • Every regulatory and compliance requirement is satisfied
  • Communication remains clear and consistent throughout the project lifecycle
