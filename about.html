About Aimco International Limited

Leading clearance and freight forwarding company in South Sudan since 2015

Who We Are

Established in 2015, Aimco International is one of the leading freight forwarding companies in South Sudan, specializing in project logistics and relocation management.

We deliver cost-effective solutions throughout South Sudan, Kenya, Uganda and Rwanda. Our comprehensive service plans are tailored to meet specific requirements of customers by learning their exact objectives regarding internal and external requirements.

Our tried and proven multimodal transportation routes to all the states and other East African countries ensure international reach whatever your cargo and timeframe is.

Quality Statement

Quality is not an option, but a necessity to survive and thrive in an environment of competition. We aim to provide total customer satisfaction that will lead to customer loyalty.

By using Total Quality Management techniques, we can locate waste, identify its causes and eliminate it. A good example of waste is rework, which is often costlier than doing the job right in the first place.

The 1-10-100 Rule

If it takes one unit of costs or effort to complete a job correctly, it will take 10 times that effort to correct an error before it reaches the customer. And once it has reached the customer, it will take 100 times the cost and effort to correct the situation, not to mention the loss of customer goodwill.
