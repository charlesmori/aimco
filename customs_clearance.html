The Customs Clearance Challenge

Complex procedures can lead to costly delays even after goods arrive

The Problem

Regardless of how quickly shipments reach their destination, complex customs procedures and documentation requirements can result in:

  • Indefinite delays at ports of entry
  • Heavy storage and parking fees
  • Missed delivery deadlines
  • Additional demurrage charges
  • Financial losses and operational disruptions

Our Solution

Aimco's comprehensive customs clearance services eliminate delays and extra costs:

  • Expert staff trained in South Sudan's complex customs procedures
  • Documentation prepared well in advance of deadlines
  • Full responsibility for customs clearance process
  • Strategic warehousing network across South Sudan
  • Swift clearance at point of arrival
